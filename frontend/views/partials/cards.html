How it works

Pick the date

You want to watch a movie on friday? No problem, all you have to do is to select the time when you want to book it.

Select the movie

Once you have decided what day you want to see a movie, a list of all the movies from that day will be shown to you. All you have to do is to chose one that you like!

The seats

After the first two steps, you now have to chose the seats that you like. You can select any seats you want as long as they're available. Now you just have to click a button and you are ready to go!
